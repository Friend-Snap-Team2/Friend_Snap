Help & Support

Welcome to the Friend Snap help page. Here you can learn how to use the app and what to do if something goes wrong.

📷 How to Add a Photo

Tap the Add Photo button in the bottom menu. Choose a photo from your device and upload it to share it with your friends.

👥 How Friends Work

Visit the Friends page to see friend suggestions and your current friends. Once someone is your friend, you can chat with them.

💬 Commenting on Photos

Each photo allows one comment per friend. Press the Comment on Picture button below a photo to leave your message.

⚠️ Reporting a Problem

If you see something that makes you uncomfortable, please report it immediately. You can contact the support team using the Details below.
